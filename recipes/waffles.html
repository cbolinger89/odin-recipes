Cinnamon Roll Waffles

Delicious Cinnamon Roll Pancakes to Start the Weekend!

My family passed down this recipe over thousands of years. We discovered it in the attic last year.

Please enjoy!

Ingredients

    Eggs
    Milk
    Flour
    Cinnamon
    Sugar

Steps

    Mix the wet ingredients
    Mix the dry ingredients besides the cinnamon
    Cook on the griddle
    Enjoy!
